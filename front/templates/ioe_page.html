IOE


Institute of Engineering (IOE) is an organization that falls under TU that conducts entrance examination where only those candidate who passes the entrance examination are deemed eligible for the admission. According to the rank of these candidates in the examination, it is decided the selection of TU engineering colleges that one can apply for. The following are the list of governmental engineering colleges that offers full( only some thousand rupees throughout the 4 year Bachelors course) and partial (3 lakh rupees throughout the 4 years Bachelors Course):
  • 1. Paschimanchal Campus
  • 2. Purwanchal Campus: Institute of Engineering
  • 3. Thapathali Campus: Institute of Engineering
  • 4. Pulchowk Campus, Institute of Engineering (IOE)
