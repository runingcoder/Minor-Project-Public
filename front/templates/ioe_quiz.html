IOE

Institute of Engineering (IOE) is an organization that falls under TU that conducts entrance examination where only those candidate who passes the entrance examination are deemed eligible for the admission. According to the rank of these candidates in the examination, it is decided the selection of TU engineering colleges that one can apply for. The following is the list of the subjects with their respectives marks distribution:
  • Physics : 45
  • Chemistry : 25
  • Maths: 50
  • English: 20
