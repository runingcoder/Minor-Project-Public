IOM

As recorded, TU is the first University in Nepal to conduct medical courses in Nepal. Institute of Medicine(IOM) was established in 1972 under TU with the aim of training all categories of health manpower required in the country. This is a course of 4 years plus 1 year of internship. As for the entrance exam, students who have passed Science PLus 2 in biology section with subjects Physics, Chemistry, Botany, Zoology and English can apply for the entrance for the selection process in IOM. There are different exam syllbaus patterns for different universities as IOM falls under TU. Zoology, Botany , Chemistry and Physics are the subjects that are in the syllabus of IOM entrance examination with the marks distribution as follows:

  • Zoology: 40
  • Botany: 40
  • Chemistry: 50
  • Physics: 50
